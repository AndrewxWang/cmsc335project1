About Keshi

This website is about Vietnamese-American singer, songwriter, producer Casey Luong (November 4, 1994)
who is better known as Keshi. Keshi is known for his lo-fi, R&B, bedroom pop style songs that collectively
combines these genres to form his own unique sound.

Fun Facts

  • Before pursuing a career in music, Keshi was an oncology nurse at the Texas Medical Center at Houstin.
  • Keshi does not like having other artists featured in his albums (but does not mind
    featuring in other people's albums).
  • Keshi has been in a long-term relationship with his best friend, Mai, since meeting her
    in middle school. She is also the voice of his producer tag he commonly uses in his songs.
